About Me

Space is not the setting (real or logical) in which things are arranged, but the means whereby the positing of things becomes possible.

Merleau Ponty

Hi, I am Neha Pokhrel, Architect/landscape Architect from Boise,Idaho. As a landscape architect, I believe in the concept of socially restorative urbanism. I like the idea of bringing social values into design of urban spaces and moving the design focus towards it. My designs philosophy has always adopted the culture that brings together people, interactions, environment and contexts. For me sustainability is the key and it can only be only achieved by bringing people in planning process.

Rethinking West End

Rethinking west end addresses the redevelopment potential for Boise’s West End neighborhood. Despite being near to the downtown Boise, West End area lacks its identity.The concept of sustainable redesign of the neighborhood was addressed by the concept of seed blocks. Analogous to a plant seed that is capable of developing into another large plant, the design concept of seed blocks is based on organic growth. Different development phases were proposed based on the design concept.

Park Design

This is a schematic diagram of conceptual framework that I designed for a park.The design had to accommodate diverse group of people living in relatively new settlement. The design constituted a central open pitch playground for multifunctional use. Traditional seating (Patti) were provided for children and elderly people. For vistors and casual park goers joggers track was also provided. To avoid noise coming from busy streets, proper selection of trees were planted along the site of the park.
